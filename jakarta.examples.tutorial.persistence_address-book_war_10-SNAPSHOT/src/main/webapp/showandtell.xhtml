Fun Facts

  • The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar on August 27, 1896, lasting only 38-45 minutes.
  • Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't.
  • The Eiffel Tower can be 15 cm taller during the summer due to thermal expansion of the iron.
  • Cows have best friends and can become stressed when they are separated.
  • Octopuses have three hearts: two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.
  • The inventor of the frisbee was turned into a frisbee. Walter Morrison, the inventor, was cremated, and his ashes were turned into a frisbee after he died.
